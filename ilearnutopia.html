Whereas many technologies disrupt through innovation and impact, iLearnutopia disrupts through mockery and satire. Using technology for the sake of using technology does not meaningfully impact learning. The internet provides access and opportunities for interaction unavailable in other forms of media, so why use it the same way you would a textbook? Inspired by the Stupid Hackathon (warning: NSFW) which aims to disrupt the idea of hackathons (trying to "hack" world hunger or income inequality in a weekend is ridiculous), this site aims to satirize those who solve problems that don't exist, those who use technology without purpose, and those who never ask, "why?"
